Reference & Data Notes

  • 32 nuclides: Cornejo et al. (2015) provides published comparison values. Operational constants may differ because they use more recent ICRP 107 nuclear decay data and ICRU 57 conversion coefficients.
  • Ga-68: addition beyond Cornejo et al., calculated using ICRP 107 photon data and ICRU 57 / ICRP 74 coefficients.
  • Y-90: pure β⁻ emitter; photon dose-rate constants are not applicable on this page. Dose offers a separate, explicitly experimental container-dependent bremsstrahlung scenario.
  • 6 therapy/imaging additions (2026): Zr-89, Cu-67, Tb-161, Ho-166, Ra-223+daughters and Ac-225+daughters — calculated from ICRP 107 photon data (ICRU 57 coefficients). For the two alpha-therapy chains the constants include progeny in secular equilibrium (standard for aged sources; equilibrium within hours). Ra-223+progeny agrees within 6% with the published conservative constant (~50 μSv·h⁻¹·GBq⁻¹·m²).
  • H*(10) and H'(0.07) are both AREA-monitoring operational quantities and are always shown side by side: H*(10) estimates effective dose, H'(0.07) indicates skin/extremity dose. Neither is selected automatically from distance, and neither bounds the other (H*(10) is not always the higher figure — Pd-103 is 40% lower). Personal dosimetry quantities are Hp(10) / Hp(0.07) / Hp(3); limit comparisons in the dose page are indicative screening only.
  • Exemption / clearance values: activity-concentration values for exemption of practices or clearance of materials under RD 1217/2024 Annex IV Table A.1. In Decay they are used specifically for a single-nuclide clearance estimate.
  • Liquid effluent column: individual concentration level per nuclide, calculated from IS-28 Annex II II.A.4 using ICRP 119 Annex F adult ingestion coefficients: (1 mSv / e(g)) / 600 L. This is one condition of II.A.4. A complete discharge assessment must also verify the mixture sum of fractions ≤ 1 and the annual activity limits (10 GBq H-3, 1 GBq C-14, < 1 GBq the rest combined), which this app does not evaluate.
  • The JSON database (data/nuclides.json) can be edited directly to update or add values.
