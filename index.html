Reference & Data Notes
  • 32 nuclides: primary source from Cornejo et al. (2015). Some values differ from published Cornejo due to more recent ICRP 107 nuclear decay data.
  • 2 nuclides: Ga-68 and Y-90 are additions beyond Cornejo et al. Ga-68 calculated using ICRU 57 / ICRP 74. Y-90: container bremsstrahlung estimates following the methodology of Zanzonico et al. (J Nucl Med 40(6), 1999); container-specific values are not tabulated in that publication.
  • 6 therapy/imaging additions (2026): Zr-89, Cu-67, Tb-161, Ho-166, Ra-223+daughters and Ac-225+daughters — calculated from ICRP 107 photon data (ICRU 57 coefficients). For the two alpha-therapy chains the constants include progeny in secular equilibrium (standard for aged sources; equilibrium within hours). Ra-223+progeny agrees within 6% with the published conservative constant (~50 μSv·h⁻¹·GBq⁻¹·m²).
  • ΓH'(0.07) used for extremity dose (d < 25 cm) only when H'(0.07) > H*(10) and all photon energies < 300 keV; otherwise H*(10) is conservatively applied.
  • Published Cornejo values preserved in cornejo_validation field for audit trail. Differences from Cornejo indicate use of updated nuclear data.
  • Clearance levels: RD 1217/2024 Tabla A.1 (niveles de autorización para la exención de materiales radiactivos del control regulatorio).
  • Liquid effluent limits calculated from IS-28 Annex II II.A.4 using ICRP 119 Annex F adult ingestion coefficients: (1 mSv / e(g)) / 600 L.
  • The JSON database (data/nuclides.json) can be edited directly to update or add values.
