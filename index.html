Reference & Data Notes
  • Cornejo et al. — primary source for ΓH*(10) values for 32 nuclides.
  • ΓH'(0.07) values in parentheses from Cornejo et al. apply when all photon energies < 300 keV and H'(0.07) > H*(10). Used for extremity dose estimation.
  • Ga-68 constant calculated using ICRU 57 / ICRP 74 methodology. Y-90 constant from Zanzonico et al. (J Nucl Med 40(6), 1999) — bremsstrahlung in 10 mm PMMA container.
  • Liquid effluent limits calculated from IS-28 Annex II II.A.4 using ICRP 119 Annex F adult ingestion coefficients: (1 mSv / e(g)) / 600 L.
