ICRP 107 Database Validation
Internal regulatory compliance check. Validates parser accuracy, Z extraction, and dose constants against ICRP-107 and Cornejo reference data. Phase 2 Validation: 15 radionuclides (7 original + 8 expanded, including I-125).
⚙️ Parser & Data Validation
Scope: This validation page is for internal use only. It verifies that:
  • Z (Atomic Number): Correctly extracted from nuclide symbol
  • Half-life: Accurately parsed from FORTRAN fixed-width NDX format
  • Photon count: Correctly filtered (G/X/AQ, E≥20keV, yield≥0.01%)
  • Max photon energy: Used for H'(0.07) assessment
  • Dose constants: Calculated from ICRP-107 photons vs published Cornejo values
