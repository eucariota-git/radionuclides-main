    Note: Estimates for radiation protection using point-source geometry. Shielding uses the Archer broad-beam method (Monte Carlo, Oumano et al. 2025) for Tc-99m, F-18, I-131 and Lu-177 — all other nuclides use spectrum-weighted narrow-beam attenuation with build-up (dose-weighted sum over the ICRP 107 photon lines with point-isotropic exposure buildup factors, ANSI/ANS-6.4.3 reference data — accounts for beam hardening and scatter buildup; infinite-medium buildup values are used — NUREG/CR-5740 discusses finite-slab and interface corrections that can move the result in either direction). Geometry, scatter, and source distribution effects are not modelled. Not suitable for dosimetric treatment planning.
